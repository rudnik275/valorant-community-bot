Valorant Console
Community Bot

A public Telegram bot for the Valorant console community (PS5, Xbox). Any Telegram supergroup can add the bot; opted-in members receive short positive messages when they hit a personal gameplay milestone — nothing more. Currently in closed alpha with one community of ~30 console players ahead of public launch.

Public on Telegram Mini App + RSO opt-in Console (PS5 / Xbox) Non-commercial

Privacy first

Players opt in via Riot Sign-On (RSO). No data is collected from non-opted-in members. One-tap opt-out in the Mini App.

Coordination

A simple opted-in member roster in the Telegram Mini App so groupmates can see who's around and tap to start a chat. No K/D, no rank, no comparisons.

Personal moments

Short congratulations for personal milestones — first Ace, personal-best ACS, rank promotion. Hard limits: max 2 per day per group. Honor only, never shame.

What this bot does not do

  • NeverPublic leaderboards or scoreboards
  • NeverPlayer comparisons or head-to-head stats
  • NeverMMR / ELO calculator or ranking alternative
  • NeverScouting tools or opponent analysis
  • NeverCharging money or paid tiers
  • NeverSharing data outside the private group

Compliance with Riot policies

  • AuthRiot Sign-On (RSO) opt-in for every member
  • EndpointsOnly documented Riot APIs via X-Riot-Token
  • ConsoleVAL-CONSOLE-MATCH-V1 / VAL-CONSOLE-RANKED-V1
  • ToneHonor and glorify only, never shame players
  • RatePer-user effective rate well under 1 req/min
  • BrandingNo Riot logos, no Valorant trade dress
